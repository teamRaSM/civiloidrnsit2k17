About Us

Civiloid is a technical event of the Department of Civil Engineering, RNS Institute of Technology to be held on 5th and 6th April 2017 under the aegis of the ACET, RNS Institute of Technology. Civiloid aims to offer a diverse range of competitions, workshops and guest lectures. The sole objective of Civiloid is to promote and encourage ideas and innovation in the field of civil engineering and it strives to present a platform to the civil engineering students to work and interact with several civil engineering aspects. We at Civiloid, do not aim to just put up an event, our vision is to reach every interested student, professor, personnel from the industry and bring them together, pool their resources together to bring up solutions to the most important challenge in India’s development – infrastructure. We focus to create a platform for the students to showcase their talent in civil engineering and help them shape a good career for themselves in this field, we envision a fest which is uniquely different from any other college event of the kind, with a strong motive and purpose.
